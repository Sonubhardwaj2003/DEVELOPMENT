  2. Can I get admission in B.Tech in ABESEC on JEE Mains and CUET-UG scores?

    Yes, you can get admission in B.Tech courses in ABESEC, Ghaziabad based on your JEE Mains score but CUET-UG scores are not required for B.Tech admission. Additionally, you will need to have scored at least 75% in your class 12th boards.

  3. What is the fee structure for various programs at ABESEC Ghaziabad?

    The fee structure at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad varies course by course. For B.Tech, the first year fee is 1.33 Lacs while for M.Tech it is 1.33 Lacs per year. The fee per year for MCA is 1.36 Lakhs.

  4. Does ABESEC Ghaziabad provide hostel facilities?

    Yes, ABESEC Ghaziabad offers great hostel facilities with both AC and non-AC rooms available to the students. There are separate hostel facilities for girls and boys and each hostel has a separate mess, library, and canteen as well as wifi facilities.

  5. Is ABESEC Ghaziabad affiliated with any university?

    ABESEC Ghaziabad is affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucky. Additionally, all the courses offered by the college are AICTE-approved.
