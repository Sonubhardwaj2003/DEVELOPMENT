About ABES engineering College

ABES Engineering College (ABES EC) is a well-known private engineering institution located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. Established in 2000, the college is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).


Key Features:
1. Courses Offered: ABES offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. Popular programs include B.Tech, MBA, and MCA. The college has departments in branches like Computer Science, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical, Electrical, and Civil Engineering.


2. Campus: The college has a modern campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including well-equipped labs, classrooms, libraries, and sports facilities. The campus provides a conducive environment for academic and extracurricular activities.


3. Accreditations and Rankings: ABES Engineering College is known for its NAAC accreditation and has been ranked among the top private engineering colleges in Uttar Pradesh by several educational surveys. It is also recognized for its quality teaching, research, and industry collaboration.


4. Placements: EC has a dedicated training and placement cell that works to secure job opportunities for students. Major recruiters include companies from IT, software, electronics, and manufacturing sectors such as Infosys, Wipro, TCS, HCL, and many others.


5. Extracurriculars: college encourages a balanced development of students by promoting extracurricular activities, including technical clubs, cultural events, sports, and leadership development programs.


6. Location: Situated in Ghaziabad, which is part of the National Capital Region (NCR), the college benefits from proximity to Delhi and Noida, offering better industrial exposure and opportunities for students.
